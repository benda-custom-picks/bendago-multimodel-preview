FAQ

Simple answers about fitment, product pages, secure checkout and support.

Are these parts compatible with my selected Benda model?

Each product page shows the available photos, price and product presentation before checkout.

Which parts change the look of a custom Benda the most?

The most visible upgrades are usually the headlight fairing, seat, compact side bags, front protection parts and small black finishing details.

How to avoid ordering the wrong part?

Compare the product photos, price and details before buying. If in doubt, ask before ordering.

Why use Benda Custom Picks?

The goal is to gather visible and useful accessory ideas for Benda Napoleon, Dark Flag V4, custom 125 and bobber motorcycles, so you do not have to search for every part separately.

How does secure payment work?

Add one or several Benda custom parts to cart, confirm your delivery details, then choose secure Stripe card checkout. Orders are processed after payment confirmation. Delivery is included unless stated otherwise. For EU delivery, no import duties are added at checkout. Outside the EU, any local import duties, taxes or carrier handling fees remain the customer’s responsibility.
