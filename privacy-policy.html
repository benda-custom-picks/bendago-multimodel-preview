Privacy Policy

Benda Custom Picks is an independent French registered business offering selected custom parts and upgrade looks for Benda Napoleon 125/250, Napoleon 450/500 and Dark Flag V4 riders.

Short version: we only collect the information needed to answer your request, prepare your order, process secure payment, follow delivery and improve the website. We do not sell your personal data.

2. What personal data we collect

Depending on how you use the website, contact us or place an order, we may collect:

  • identity and contact data: name, email address and message content;
  • order data: selected products, quantities, colour options, cart details, motorcycle model and order comments;
  • delivery data: delivery address and delivery information needed to follow the order;
  • payment status data: payment confirmation, payment reference and payment provider information, but not full card numbers or card security codes;
  • technical and analytics data: page views, clicks, traffic source, device type, approximate location, browser information and checkout events.

4. Payments

Payments are processed securely by Stripe. We do not store your full card number or card security code on our website. Stripe may process payment data under its own privacy and security rules.

Where a customer is offered or selects Klarna at Stripe checkout, Klarna may process the payment, eligibility and repayment information necessary to provide its payment service under its own privacy and security rules. Benda Custom Picks receives only the order and payment-status information needed to manage the order, accounting, support and disputes.

5. Cookies, Google Tag Manager and Google Analytics

We use Google Tag Manager and Google Analytics 4 to understand website traffic, product clicks, cart actions, shared cart activity and checkout flow. This helps us improve the website, product selection and customer experience.

Your browser or device may allow you to block or delete cookies. Where consent is required by applicable law, analytics cookies and similar trackers should only be used according to your choices.

6. Who receives the data

Personal data may be shared only when needed with:

  • payment providers such as Stripe and, where offered or selected at checkout, Klarna;
  • website hosting and technical providers;
  • email, form or order processing providers;
  • delivery, logistics or supplier partners when needed to process an order;
  • accounting, tax, legal or administrative authorities when required by law.

We do not sell your personal data.

7. International transfers

Some service providers may process data outside France or the European Economic Area. When this happens, we rely on appropriate safeguards required by applicable data protection law, such as adequacy decisions, contractual safeguards or provider privacy frameworks where applicable.

8. Data retention

We keep personal data only for as long as necessary for the purposes described above. Retention periods depend on the type of data, the reason for processing and applicable legal obligations.

  • customer requests: normally up to 3 years after the last contact;
  • order, payment and delivery follow-up: for the time needed to manage the order and support;
  • accounting and invoicing records: generally up to 10 years where required by law;
  • analytics data: for a limited period according to our analytics settings;
  • security and fraud data: only for as long as necessary to protect the website and customers.

9. Your rights

Under data protection law, you may have the right to:

  • access your personal data;
  • correct inaccurate or incomplete data;
  • request deletion of your data, subject to legal obligations;
  • object to certain processing;
  • request restriction of processing;
  • request data portability where applicable;
  • withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

To exercise your rights, contact us at support@bendacustompicks.com. We aim to respond as soon as possible and within the legal time limits.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the French data protection authority, the CNIL, if you believe your rights have not been respected.

10. Security

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect personal data against unauthorised access, loss, misuse or disclosure. No online service can be guaranteed to be fully risk-free.

11. Children

Our website is intended for motorcycle parts and order enquiries from adults. We do not knowingly target or collect personal data from children.

12. Independent business notice

Benda Custom Picks is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or officially connected to Benda, Keeway, MBP or their distributors.
