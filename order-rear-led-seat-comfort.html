Rear LED seat upgrade for Benda Napoleon 125/250

The Rear Led Seat Comfort + is a premium rear-end accessory for the Benda Napoleon 125/250. It combines a ribbed comfort seat with an integrated 3-in-1 rear LED strip, giving the bike a cleaner and more modern custom profile. This part is useful for riders who want the rear line to look sharper without adding bulky accessories or losing the Napoleon’s compact bobber stance.

As a Benda Napoleon custom part, it works well with front fairing, engine-side covers and chassis protection because it completes the visual line from front to rear. The result is a more finished rear profile, stronger light detail and a cleaner premium look for riders building a distinctive Napoleon 125 or 250.
